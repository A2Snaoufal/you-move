#1 Welcome to you-move.org

Posted by Federico Yankelevich on 29 March 2009

you-move.org is a social network web site for transportation.
The aim of the You Move project is to offer an open and free platform to share trips and vehicles in order to minimize the environmental footprint of transportation globally.
Movers (you-move.org users) can adapt privacy criterias to their needs choosing between private groups, corporate groups, private and public communities, public transportation and commercial services.
CarPooling: Plan your daily trip in advance to offer a seat and grab back one when you need.
CarSharing: Share communities vehicles organizing sequential one-way trips.

You Move is an attempt to improve our world. Let's try all together!
